Do you have a pool?

  1. Go to the admin page and add your pool.
  2. Share your unique pool URL with your friends.
  3. When you are going swimming and want friends to join you, press the "Go Swimming!" to notify everyone who follows your pool.

Does your friend have a pool?

  1. Have your friend send you their unique pool URL and add their pool to your "Pools" page.
  2. Make sure notifications are enabled for this app and not suppressed by your phone settings.
  3. When your friends go swimming, you will receive a notification indicating you are invited to come swim as well!
